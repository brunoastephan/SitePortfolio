rogue fungi

Rogue Fungi is a roguelike deckbuilder card game set in a magical forest corrupted by mushrooms. Create cards with your enchanted book, survive the attack of infected creatures and discover who is behind the curse.

This game was a part of Panorama Brasil in BIG 2021 Festival.

I am the only artist currently working in the project and that entails creating all the animations, HUD, VFX in the game. Some of the visual design elements are made by hand and some of them are made directly in Unity.
